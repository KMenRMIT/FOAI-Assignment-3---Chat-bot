The problem

Why we built
HealthGuide

Every day, patients leave hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies clutching prescriptions and discharge notes they don't fully understand. Complex medical jargon, dense instruction sheets, and rushed consultations leave many people confused about what to take, when, and why.

This confusion has real consequences: missed doses, incorrect self-treatment, unnecessary anxiety, and reluctance to seek further help. For people with lower health literacy, limited English, or chronic conditions, the challenge is even greater.

AI HealthGuide was created as a prototype to explore how conversational AI could make medical information more accessible — acting as a friendly bridge between patients and the healthcare system, never a replacement for it.

📊

Health literacy matters

Low health literacy is a recognised public health challenge — affecting the ability of patients to navigate healthcare, understand instructions, and make informed decisions.

Features

From prescription labels to complex diagnoses — HealthGuide translates healthcare language into plain, friendly language.

Prescription Simplifier

Paste any prescription or medication instruction and receive a clear, plain-English explanation of exactly when and how to take your medicine — with warnings where they matter.

📋

Confused by a diagnosis, test result, or medical report? Ask about any term and get an easy, jargon-free explanation tailored to everyday understanding.

Not sure what to ask at your next appointment? HealthGuide helps you prepare the right questions so you leave informed, confident, and in control of your care.

Who it helps

Built for everyone
navigating healthcare

HealthGuide is designed to support anyone who finds medical communication confusing — regardless of background, age, or literacy level.

Patients

Helps everyday patients understand prescriptions, diagnoses, and medical instructions without needing a medical degree. Reduces confusion, anxiety, and medication errors.

Our philosophy

Designed with care
at every step

Every decision in HealthGuide's design was made with patient safety, honesty, and responsible AI in mind.

01

Patient-first design

Every response is crafted to reduce confusion, not add to it. Plain language, clear structure, and friendly tone are non-negotiable — not afterthoughts.

02

Clear limitations

HealthGuide never pretends to be a doctor. Every interaction makes clear that AI explanations support — not replace — professional medical advice.

03

Emergency awareness

The system detects keywords indicating potential emergencies and immediately directs users to call 000 — because no chatbot response is worth a delayed emergency call.

04

Encouraging professional help

HealthGuide consistently encourages users to consult their doctor, pharmacist, or specialist — it opens conversations with professionals rather than ending them.

05

Transparency about AI

Users always know they are talking to an AI assistant, not a human. There is no attempt to simulate a professional relationship or provide false reassurance.

06

Responsible scope

HealthGuide handles communication and explanation only. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or make clinical recommendations — ever.

The team

Built by students,
with care

AI HealthGuide was created as an assignment project for Foundations of AI (FOAI) — a university subject exploring practical, ethical applications of artificial intelligence.

The project challenged us to think critically about how AI should — and shouldn't — be used in high-stakes domains like healthcare. Every design decision reflects that responsibility.

⚠️

AI HealthGuide is not a medical professional

This prototype is built for educational purposes only. It is not validated, regulated, or approved for clinical use. All explanations are simplified and may not apply to your specific situation. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional — your GP, pharmacist, or specialist — for any medical decision. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 000 immediately.
