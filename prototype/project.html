Project Documentation

Hybrid AI chatbot prototype for healthcare communication.

This page summarises the proposed AI product design, data strategy, evaluation plan, and future development pathway.

1

Problem

Patients often struggle to understand medical information written in complex language.

2

AI Solution

A Generative AI chatbot simplifies prescriptions, terminology, and medical instructions.

3

Data Strategy

Future development would use verified health education materials, prescription examples, and multilingual resources.

4

Evaluation

Testing would include readability, accuracy review, user understanding, bias testing, and safety validation.

5

Future Implementation

The prototype could later connect to an LLM with healthcare guardrails, human oversight, and secure data handling.
