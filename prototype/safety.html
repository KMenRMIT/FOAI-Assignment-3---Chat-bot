Responsible AI

Safety, privacy, and ethics come first.

AI HealthGuide is designed as a support tool, not a replacement for doctors, pharmacists, or emergency services.

No Diagnosis

The chatbot does not diagnose diseases, prescribe medication, or replace medical professionals.

Privacy Protection

The proposed system would use consent, data minimisation, and secure handling of sensitive information.

Bias Mitigation

The system should be tested across different age groups, cultures, and language backgrounds.

Human Oversight

Healthcare professionals should validate critical information and review system performance.

Emergency Redirection

Serious symptoms such as chest pain or breathing difficulty should trigger urgent medical advice.

Transparency

Users should clearly understand that chatbot responses are AI-generated and may be limited.
