About the Product

A responsible healthcare communication assistant.

AI HealthGuide is designed to support patients by transforming complex medical information into clearer, simpler language.

Why we created AI HealthGuide

Many patients struggle to understand prescriptions, medical terms, and healthcare instructions. This can lead to confusion, stress, and medication errors.

AI HealthGuide aims to reduce this problem by giving simple, patient-friendly explanations while encouraging users to consult healthcare professionals.

What it does

  • Simplifies medical instructions
  • Explains healthcare terminology
  • Creates patient-friendly summaries
  • Supports responsible health communication

Patients

Helps users understand basic healthcare information more clearly.

Clinics

Could reduce repeated explanation workload for common instructions.

Pharmacists

Supports medication instruction clarity without replacing professional advice.
